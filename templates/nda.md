Review Checklist:
□ Verify mutual (bilateral) confidentiality obligations
□ Check definition of "Confidential Information" — flag overbroad definitions
   that could capture publicly available information or independent work product
□ Confirm standard carve-outs: (i) publicly known, (ii) independently developed,
   (iii) rightfully received from third parties, (iv) already known prior to disclosure
□ Flag any non-compete or non-solicitation clauses — check scope and duration
□ Review term and termination provisions — confirm survival period for
   confidentiality obligations (standard: 2-5 years post-termination)
□ Verify permitted disclosure exceptions (court order, regulatory requirement)
   include notice obligation and opportunity to seek protective order
□ Check that return/destruction of materials is addressed at termination
□ Confirm governing law and dispute resolution mechanism match jurisdiction
□ Flag any indemnification provisions — unusual in standard NDAs
□ Verify no assignment without consent

Drafting Standards:
- Formal contractual tone
- Define all capitalized terms on first use
- Use "shall" for obligations, "may" for permissions
- Number all sections and subsections
- Include standard boilerplate: severability, waiver, entire agreement, counterparts
- Plain English where possible — avoid unnecessary Latin or archaic phrasing
